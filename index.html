Hello, I am a PhD student in Computer Science at University of Maryland, College Park, advised by Ming C. Lin.

My research goal is to develop techniques for applying machine learning to geometric problems in computer graphics, to enable new ways for artists and engineers to create. Research interests include: animation and simulation, geometry processing, and geometric deep learning.

I have previously interned at World Labs, Roblox and Google. Prior to that, I worked as a software engineer at Amazon. I completed my MS in Computer Science at NYU, and BA in Film Production at USC School of Cinematic Arts.
